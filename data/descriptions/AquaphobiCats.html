You are an Alien!
You have come to earth bearing the gifts of your people for our planet's most intelligent creatures.

You quickly introduce yourself to the cats of the planet.

Worried that they will lose their comfortable lifestyle if you share their secrets, they throw you into a volcano to destroy you with your one weakness, lava.

Use your Spray Bottle to stay alive as long as you can by jumping and launching yourself to rocky platforms, battling cats and their yarnballs as you go.
