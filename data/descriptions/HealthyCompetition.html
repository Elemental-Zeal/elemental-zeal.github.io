Made for Ludum Dare 45.

It's the annual master wizard chef competition! Wizard chefs from all around the globe compete in order to be ranked the new 5-star master wizard chef.
Contests race race to select their top of the line weapon to help them out in the ingredients dungeon.
Unfortunately, you get trampled and before you can get up all the weapons are taken. Looks like you're going to have to start this challenge with nothing.
