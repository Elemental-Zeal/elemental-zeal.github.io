Purplebeard the Dwarf wants to put the life of blacksmithing behind him. He dreams now only of beautiful landscaping and design.

The nearby mines are filled with riches, but have long since been abandoned after they became too dangerous to work in. Even worse, since they were abandoned, they've become infested with monsters.

Help Purplebeard defeat enemies and navigate the mines with his trusty grappling axe in order to collect gems to fund his business venture, so that he may fulfill his dreams.
