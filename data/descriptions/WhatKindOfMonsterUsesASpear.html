In What Kind of Monster Uses a Spear, you play as an adorable monster on his relaxing (and tasty) fishing day.


Play in two different fishing modes:

- Arcade mode, where you try to get as many points as you can within a three minute period, and attempt to beat your high score.

- Zen mode, where there are no time limits, and you can play the game at your own pace.

This game focuses on light, relaxing fun, and is a great way to pass the time.

We hope you enjoy What Kind of Monster Uses a Spear and its colorful art, pleasant atmosphere, and peaceful mood.
